Her Life as a writer

Anne Rice (October 10, 1941 - December 11, 2021) She went by many names as a writer but was born Howard Allen O'Brein. She changed her name to Anne in the first grade. Growing up in New Orleans with all of the elaborate cemetaries and the Vodu heritage fostered a love for the supernatural realm!

Her mother passed away in 1956 from complications of alcoholism and soon after Anne disavowed her faith.

She met and married Stan Rice at the age of 20 and soon after had a daugther named Michelle who at 5 years old passed away from Leukemia. Devastating as it was, she then began the first book in "The Vampire Chronicles", Interview with a Vampire(1976), which was written in 5 weeks.In this book, she portrayed a Michelle-like child who gained eternal life as a vampire. The book was the 1st best seller in the series.
